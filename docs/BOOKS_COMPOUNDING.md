# Advanced Compounding and Capital Growth Reading List (20)

These books focus on long-run capital growth, sizing, portfolio construction,
and compounding mechanics. They are rigorous, not promises of returns.

1) The Kelly Capital Growth Investment Criterion: Theory and Practice
   - Leonard C. MacLean, Edward O. Thorp, William T. Ziemba
2) Fortune's Formula
   - William Poundstone
3) Beat the Market
   - Edward O. Thorp, Sheen T. Kassouf
4) Portfolio Selection: Efficient Diversification of Investments
   - Harry M. Markowitz
5) Active Portfolio Management
   - Richard C. Grinold, Ronald N. Kahn
6) Expected Returns
   - Antti Ilmanen
7) Risk and Asset Allocation
   - Attilio Meucci
8) Risk Parity: A New Approach to Portfolio Management
   - Thierry Roncalli
9) Portfolio Management Formulas
   - Ralph Vince
10) The Handbook of Portfolio Mathematics
   - Ralph Vince
11) Quantitative Equity Portfolio Management
   - Ludwig B. Chincarini, Daehwan Kim
12) Asset Pricing
   - John H. Cochrane
13) Dynamic Asset Pricing Theory
   - Darrell Duffie
14) Continuous-Time Finance
   - Robert C. Merton
15) Stochastic Calculus for Finance I: The Binomial Asset Pricing Model
   - Steven E. Shreve
16) Stochastic Calculus for Finance II: Continuous-Time Models
   - Steven E. Shreve
17) The Mathematics of Financial Modeling and Investment Management
   - Sergio M. Focardi, Frank J. Fabozzi, Radu Tunaru
18) The Econometrics of Financial Markets
   - John Y. Campbell, Andrew W. Lo, A. Craig MacKinlay
19) Value at Risk: The New Benchmark for Managing Financial Risk
   - Philippe Jorion
20) Quantitative Trading: How to Build Your Own Algorithmic Trading Business
   - Ernest P. Chan

Key compounding concepts extracted (high level):
- Log-utility growth (Kelly) and fractional Kelly risk control
- Leverage constraints and drawdown-aware sizing
- Volatility targeting to stabilize compounding paths
- Estimation error and shrinkage to prevent overbetting
- Diversification benefits vs turnover and trading costs
- Regime-aware allocation and stress testing
- Risk budgeting and risk parity mechanics

Further advanced reading (optional):
- The Handbook of Fixed Income Securities — Frank J. Fabozzi
- Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives — John C. Hull
- Dynamic Asset Allocation: Modern Portfolio Theory Updated — James Picerno
- Portfolio Construction and Analytics — Frank J. Fabozzi
- The Econometrics of Financial Markets — Campbell, Lo, MacKinlay

Note: No reading list guarantees performance. These are research references only.
